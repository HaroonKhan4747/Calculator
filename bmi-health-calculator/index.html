BMI Prime & Ponderal Index Explained

BMI Prime compares your BMI to the upper limit of normal (usually 25) to show how far you are from a healthy weight. The Ponderal Index uses height cubed instead of squared, which can give a better reading for very tall or short individuals. Both tools are excellent for fine-tuning your weight assessment.
