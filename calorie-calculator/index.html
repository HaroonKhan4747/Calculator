Yes, it‚Äôs possible ‚Äî especially if you‚Äôre new to training or returning after a break. Focus on eating at maintenance or a slight deficit, get enough protein, and strength train consistently. This approach is often called body recomposition.

Absolutely. Without sufficient calories and protein, your body won‚Äôt have the energy or nutrients it needs to build new muscle tissue. Think of calories as the fuel and protein as the building blocks for growth.
