Popular Calculators

Age Calculator

Find your exact age in years, months, weeks, days, hours, minutes, and seconds. Also includes Korean and Lunar/Chinese ages, day of the week you were born, days until your next birthday, and future age calculation. (Also known as Chronological Age Calculator.)

Animal Age Calculator

Calculate your pet’s age in human years. Compare your dog’s or cat’s age to a human equivalent, and see their age progression over time.

Infant Age Calculator

Track your baby’s age in months, weeks, days, hours, and minutes.

Korean Age Calculator

Discover your age according to the traditional Korean age system, and compare with chronological age.

Lunar / Chinese Age Calculator

Calculate your age based on the lunar calendar, Western age, and find your zodiac sign.

Gestational Age Calculator

Estimate weeks of pregnancy, due date, and percentage of full-term pregnancy using LMP or conception date.

Age Difference Calculator

Calculate the exact age gap between two people in years, months, weeks, days, hours, and minutes.

Age on Mars / Other Planets

See how old you would be on Mars, Jupiter, and other planets based on your Earth date of birth.
