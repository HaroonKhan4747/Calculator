FAQs

How much do you need to survive in Dubai?

A single person typically needs AED 3,000–5,000 for essentials excluding rent. Rent varies: shared spaces AED 800–1,500+, studios AED 3,000–5,000+, one-bed AED 6,000–9,000+ depending on area.

Which Gulf country is the cheapest to live in?

Oman and Bahrain tend to be more affordable overall. Saudi can be cheaper outside major hubs. UAE and Qatar are usually higher due to housing.

Do the results include schooling?

Yes. If family size is 3 or more, the estimate adds schooling per child (varies with living type).

Are these numbers accurate?

They’re realistic estimates for guidance, not exact quotes. Real costs depend on city, neighborhood and lifestyle choices.
