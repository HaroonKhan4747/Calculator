FAQs

How much do you need to survive in Dubai?

On average, a single person may need around AED 4,000–6,000 monthly, while families may require AED 12,000+ depending on lifestyle.

Which Gulf country is the cheapest to live in?

Generally, Oman is considered more affordable compared to Dubai, Qatar, and Saudi Arabia.

Do the results include schooling?

No, schooling costs vary greatly by institution and are not included in the default calculations.
