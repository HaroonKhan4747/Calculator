FAQs

How much do you need to survive in Dubai?

A single person typically needs AED 3,000–5,000 for essentials excluding rent. Rent varies: shared spaces AED 800–1,500+, studios AED 3,000–5,000+, 1-bed AED 6,000–9,000+ depending on area.

Which Gulf country is the cheapest to live in?

Oman and Bahrain tend to be more affordable overall. Saudi can be cheaper outside major hubs. UAE and Qatar are usually the most expensive due to housing.

Do the results include schooling?

If you set family size ≥ 3, the estimate adds a schooling component for children (varies by living type). Adjust your inputs to reflect your situation.

Are these numbers accurate?

They are realistic estimates for guidance, not exact quotes. Real costs depend on city, neighborhood, and lifestyle choices.
