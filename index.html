FAQs

How much do you need to survive in Dubai?

A single person typically needs $800–1,500 USD for essentials excluding rent. Rent varies: shared spaces $200–400, studios $800–1,500+, 1-bed $1,500–2,500 depending on area.

Which Gulf country is the cheapest to live in?

Oman and Bahrain tend to be the most affordable. Saudi can be cheaper outside major hubs. UAE and Qatar are generally the most expensive due to housing costs.

Do the results include schooling?

If family size ≥ 3, the estimate adds schooling for children (varies by living type). Adjust inputs to reflect your situation.

Are these numbers exact?

They are realistic estimates for planning purposes, not exact quotes. Real costs depend on city, neighborhood, and lifestyle choices.
