FAQs

How much do you need to survive in Dubai?

On average, a single person needs around $1,000–$1,500 per month excluding rent.

Which Gulf country is the cheapest to live in?

Oman and Bahrain are generally considered more affordable compared to UAE and Qatar.

Can I use this calculator for all Gulf countries?

Yes, it covers UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Oman, Kuwait, and Bahrain.
