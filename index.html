How much do you need to survive in Dubai (UAE)?

On average, a single person spends AED 3,500–6,000 monthly depending on lifestyle.

Which Gulf country is the cheapest to live in?

Oman and Bahrain generally have lower living costs compared to Dubai and Qatar.

Do the results include schooling?

Yes, school costs are included in the estimate if you select family size more than 1.

Is Dubai more expensive than Saudi Arabia?

Yes, Dubai is costlier in rent and lifestyle compared to most Saudi cities.

What currency is used in the calculator?

You can select AED, SAR, QAR, OMR, BHD, or KWD depending on the country.

Can this help me plan relocation?
