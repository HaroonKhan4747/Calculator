About GulfExpense

GulfExpense is a minimal, fast tool that helps you estimate monthly living costs across Gulf countries. Choose a country, family size, living type, currency, and your salary to see a detailed breakdown and whether your income covers typical expenses.

We focus on clarity, speed, and realistic starting points. Costs are estimates and will vary with city, neighborhood, and lifestyle choices.
